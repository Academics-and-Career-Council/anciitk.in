About the Convention

Since its inception, IIT Kanpur has been at the forefront of many technological and scientific advancements in the past and keeps on making strides with its exceptional research culture. This stature could not have been achieved without the expertise of our faculty, and an equal dedication and excellence of our students. With this thought at heart, SRC is a kind effort to bring to light the exceptional research contributions being made by students, over the nation.

A PLATFORM FOR RESEARCHERS

SRC aims to foster student-based research and create a platform for young researchers to meet and exchange ideas. Being the first of its kind, SRC brings together the best college students from all over the country to present their innovations and experience the current research culture across the country.

A PLETHORA OF EVENTS

Apart from the student conferences, SRC encompasses talks, panel discussions, workshops, academic competitions, technical exhibitions, and sessions covering various aspects of research and its implications in the growth and development of Industry, Society and Economy of the nation.

DSIRE

This year, at SRC'21:
Let's Explore Research in Data Science !

Data is the new oil and the tools to handle it are turning out to be the tools that shape our future. With high end computational capabilities becoming much more easily available, extensive study of Data has penetrated each and every aspect of present humanlife. Starting from Particle Physics and Microbiology, going up to Astrophysics, there is no paradigm left today that does not utilize data manipulation tools. With Data gaining so much importance in the daily lives of us, it is impossible to envision a future without understanding it. Hence, SRC'21, through it's theme and stage, calls for budding researchers and scientists to "D.S.I.R.E"

Theme Talks Organised Under SRC

SRC has organised a number of talks from time to time in order to promote the spirit of research among the students and the general public.

S p a R E

  • The exponential progress in the field of Space Research directly points out the necessity to create awareness and motivate the students to pursue research with applications attesting to the domain of Space Sciences.

  • With this thought at heart, SRC'20 proudly presents the newest addition to our list of enlightening and impactful theme talks-SpaRE (Space and Research)

  • Experts from various domains of space research - from Rocket Propulsion and Launch Vehicle Designs to exo-planetary explorations, shall talk and interact with the students on different aspects of space research, it's scope, potential opportunities and enlighten the audience on how these awesome and successful space missions are planned and executed!

B E E R

BECAUSE EVEN ENTREPRENEURS RESEARCH

A story about the role that a novel research work plays in making a startup idea into a brand value product and the journey of transformation of innovative research ideas into leading future corporate firms.

W i R E


WOMEN IN RESEARCH

An initiative to appreciate the impact that eminent women personalities have created in the fields of research and the role that they have played, in sync, to shape the growth and development of the R&D, Industry, and Economy of the nation.

D a R E


DEFENCE AND RESEARCH

A walk-through of the impact that research can bring up in strengthening the defense sector and the role that R&D plays in establishing the best of the defense systems, that India in itself has been able to achieve.

I n V A R


INDUSTRIAL vs ACADEMIC RESEARCH

An event that celebrates the Eco-system of R&D and industry and brings together leading industrialists and exceptional Academicians to discuss upon the possibilities and challenges in collaborations between the two domains.
